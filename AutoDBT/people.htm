The project involves researchers at the University of Arizona (UA) and Washington State University (WSU).
  • Bedirhan Urgun was the lead programmer. Bedirhan graduates from WSU in May, 2003 with a Master's of Computer Science thesis and now lives in Turkey.
  • Jessica K. Miller was also a lead programmer. Jessica is a Doctoral student at the University of Washington.
  • Kyriacos Pavlou is currently the lead programmer. Kyriacos is a student at the University of Arizona.
  • Professor Richard T. Snodgrass (UA) is co-directing the project.
  • Assistant Professor Curtis Dyreson (WSU) is co-directing the project. Curtis also maintains the web pages.
